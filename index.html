About Bimax

Bimax Branding World is a company that was founded by 3 self driven individuals. It came about due to the need from many companies who were starting up and needed to build there brand in terms of marketing and also show-casing their brand both graphically and visually. When the company started we first did not target high-end clients but we focused on retail of single items as a basis of starting but as time passed the business grew and we started getting ready to scale up and expand as a business and now serve more clients including top stock companies who have switched their branding partners and opted for us. They were determined to bring out the best in most companies and that is how the company was started.

What we offer:
  1. Cloth Branding
  2. Mug and Plate Branding
  3. Graphic Design
